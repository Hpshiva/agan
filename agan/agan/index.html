REGAIN – A Wellness Program for Corporates

Agan Health introduces REGAIN, a wellness program for corporates in India, in collaboration with Niraa Wellness, a top holistic healthcare institution in Sakleshpura, Karnataka. The 2-day wellness retreat offers a quick and effective way for employers to guide their employees towards holistic health. This corporate employee wellness program offers a combination of mental and physical healthcare therapies like Naturopathy, Yoga, Counselling, Mindfulness and other practices that contribute to boosting employee morale.

Why Choose Our Wellness Retreat?

Serene Location

Organised in Sakleshpura, amidst the lush greens, the 2-day corporate employee wellness retreat promotes a peaceful environment that facilitates health and well-being. Gift your employees the opportunity to recharge and revitalise in the lap of nature and boost their morale while practising mindfulness and team-building activities.

Experienced Practitioners

Our retreat program is curated by experienced physical and mental healthcare specialists. The practitioners have decades of experience helping C-level executives, employees, and individuals achieve physical, mental, and emotional stability through customised techniques honed to perfection.

Personalised Activities

We bring a personalised holistic wellness approach to your team through team building, counselling, holistic therapies, mindfulness exercises, and meditation workshops. The retreat offers a path for self-discovery and inner peace during its course and will boost team morale with alternative activities for those who can’t take part in them.

Diet Management

During this 2-day retreat, breakfast, lunch, and dinner diet plans with refreshments have been carefully selected to maximise the effectiveness of activities. A sattvic diet comprising fruits, vegetables, and whole grains provides healthy and natural energy to the body, promoting the intake of essential nutrients without harming the body.
